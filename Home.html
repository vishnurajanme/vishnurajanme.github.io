Dr. Vishnu Rajan

I hail from Edanad, an Island surrounded by rivers created by god’s magic situated in the lush green Alapuzha District of Kerala, India.
I focus on Signal Processing as my research area and completed PhD in the same, even though I like embedded systems and Robotics. Love to guide projects and crave in making it the best one. Most of the students whom I have guided has got some kind of recognition in project/paper presentations.
Getting updated with new trends in technology is my favourite hobby. Apart from that I love to code in python, develop android apps and also like to experiment with new algorithms in AI and ML.
Presently working as Associate Professor at Sahrdaya College of Engineering & Technology, affiliated under Kerala Technological University, heading the department of Electronics & Communication and enjoying my life here.
